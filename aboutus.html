About Us

Our Collective Closet was started by a group of female high school students in the middle of prom season. We realized that the expensive cost of prom is a barrier for some students to enjoy their senior year, and we wanted to make sure that people have a way of obtaining clothing items for free. We decided that our service should not be limited to just formal wear, so it can be utilized by everybody. We hope that the users find our trading system useful and enjoyable!
